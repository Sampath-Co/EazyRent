Lease Agreement

Please review the following rules and regulations before proceeding:

  • The tenant shall not sublet the property without prior written consent from the landlord.
  • The tenant is responsible for maintaining the property in good condition and reporting any damages promptly.
  • Rent must be paid on or before the due date each month.
  • Any modifications to the property must be approved by the landlord in writing.
  • Pets are not allowed unless explicitly permitted in the lease agreement.
  • Noise levels must be kept to a minimum to avoid disturbing neighbors.
  • The tenant must comply with all local laws and regulations during the lease period.
